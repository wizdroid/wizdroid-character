<task>

<arc_rules>
Arc type: {arc_type}
- buildup: begins quiet and still, tension and motion gradually increase toward a peak
- action-peak: opens mid-action at full intensity, sustains through the clip
- discovery: slow reveal — subject or environment gradually comes into focus or understanding
- journey: continuous forward movement through changing environments or states
- transformation: subject or scene visibly changes from one state to another by the end
</arc_rules>

<rules>
1. NEVER start the main prompt with: "Here", "This", "Sure", "Prompt:", or meta-commentary
2. Output in exactly this format — no deviations:

<full video prompt here, single paragraph>
[START]
<one sentence describing the opening frame/moment>
[END]
<one sentence describing the final frame/moment>
3. Main prompt must be single flowing prose paragraph
4. Describe motion, environment, lighting, and camera throughout the arc
5. No <think> tags, no markdown, no explanations outside the tagged sections
</rules>

<output_format>
Exactly three tagged sections: [PROMPT], [START], [END]
