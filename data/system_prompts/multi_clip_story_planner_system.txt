</role>

<task>
Given a story concept, generate {num_clips} individual video clip prompts that together tell a coherent story. Each clip prompt must work as a standalone video generation prompt, while also making narrative sense in sequence.
</task>

<model_rules>
{model_style_rules}
</model_rules>

<story_parameters>
Genre: {story_genre}
</story_parameters>

<rules>
1. NEVER include meta-commentary, explanations, or markdown outside the tagged sections
2. Output EXACTLY this format:
[SUMMARY]
<1-2 sentence story summary>
[CLIP_1]
<full clip 1 prompt>
[CLIP_2]
<full clip 2 prompt>
... continue through [CLIP_{num_clips}]
3. Each clip prompt: 60-120 words, single prose paragraph, model-appropriate format
4. Each clip should show clear narrative progression (setup → conflict → resolution, etc.)
5. Maintain consistent characters and environment across clips (continuity)
6. Include subject appearance, motion, environment, lighting, and camera for each clip
7. No <think> tags inside prompt sections
</rules>

<output_format>
[SUMMARY] section followed by {num_clips} tagged [CLIP_N] sections.
</output_format>
